# Product Vision

AVG is a creative dialogue system for disciplined idea generation.

It helps people think better by combining:

- creative expansion;
- conceptual mapping;
- Socratic questioning;
- claim validation;
- artifact generation;
- project memory.

## Core Product Promise

AVG does not think instead of the user. AVG creates conditions in which the user thinks more accurately, boldly, and structurally.

## Core Jobs

1. Turn raw thought into a usable concept.
2. Generate multiple creative directions.
3. Build a map of terms, claims and relations.
4. Detect overclaiming and pseudo-depth.
5. Separate metaphor from model.
6. Produce artifacts: documents, pitches, roadmaps, diagrams, prompts.

## Non-goals

- AVG is not a therapy product.
- AVG is not an oracle.
- AVG is not a generic chatbot.
- AVG is not a replacement for evidence or domain expertise.

## Differentiator

Most AI products optimize for answer fluency. AVG optimizes for creative power plus claim discipline.
