# No Fairy Tale Judge

Score the answer from 0 to 1.

Fail if:

- strong words are undefined;
- metaphor is treated as fact;
- there is no mechanism;
- the answer sounds profound but is not actionable;
- the answer claims certainty beyond evidence.

Reward:

- clear scope;
- useful creativity;
- explicit uncertainty;
- next actions;
- claim status markers.
