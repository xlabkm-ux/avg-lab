# Context Pack: Validation

The validation system protects AVG from:

- metaphor-as-fact;
- strong words without definitions;
- map/territory substitution;
- fake certainty;
- level mixing;
- access-mode mixing;
- social confirmation as proof;
- fairy tale answers.

Claim statuses:

- definition;
- working_distinction;
- operational_marker;
- indirect_sign;
- hypothesis;
- metaphor_only;
- prohibited_positive_claim;
- boundary_statement.
